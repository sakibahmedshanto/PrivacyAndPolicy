# Privacy Policy

**PrepaidPro – DESCO Meter Tracker**  
**Last updated: June 19, 2026**

---

## 1. Introduction

PrepaidPro ("we", "our", or "us") operates the PrepaidPro – DESCO Meter Tracker mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App. By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Meter account number** — the DESCO prepaid meter number you enter to track your balance.

### 2.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device push token (FCM token)** — a unique identifier generated by your device used solely to deliver balance alert notifications to you.
- **Language preference** — the display language you select (English or Bengali).
- **Usage data** — electricity consumption patterns derived from your meter's recharge history, processed locally on your device to generate predictions and insights.

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

We do not collect:

- Your name, email address, or phone number
- Payment or financial information
- Precise location or GPS data
- Contact list or call logs
- Any data from other apps on your device

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- Display your DESCO prepaid meter balance and recharge history
- Send you low-balance push notifications based on your configured threshold
- Generate AI-powered consumption predictions and recharge suggestions
- Improve app performance and fix bugs
- Respond to your support requests

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

- Your meter account number, device push token, and notification preferences are stored securely on our backend (Supabase) solely to enable push notifications.
- All communication between the App and our backend is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS.
- Your recharge history and usage analytics are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.
- We retain your data only as long as you use the App. You can request deletion at any time (see Section 8).

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policy:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---|---|---|
| **Firebase Cloud Messaging (Google)** | Delivering push notifications | https://policies.google.com/privacy |
| **DESCO API** | Fetching your meter balance and recharge data | https://www.desco.org.bd |
| **Supabase** | Storing push notification registration data | https://supabase.com/privacy |

We only share your device push token and meter account number with these services to the minimum extent necessary to operate the App.

---

## 6. Permissions

The App requests the following device permissions:

| Permission | Reason |
|---|---|
| **POST_NOTIFICATIONS** | To send you balance alert notifications |
| **INTERNET** | To fetch your meter balance from DESCO and deliver notifications |
| **RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED** | To restore scheduled notifications after device restart |

We do not request access to your camera, microphone, contacts, or storage.

---

## 7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

---

## 8. Your Rights and Data Deletion

You have the right to:

- **Access** the data we hold about you.
- **Delete** your data — uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data. To delete your push notification registration from our servers, contact us at the email below.
- **Opt out of notifications** — you can disable notifications at any time through your device settings or within the App's settings screen.
